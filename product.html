What is Organic food?

Organic foods are agricultural products that are produced using methods that prioritize environmental sustainability and the preservation of natural resources. These foods are grown without the use of synthetic chemicals such as pesticides, herbicides, and fertilizers. Organic foods are grouped into 3;fruits,vegetables and diary products.
